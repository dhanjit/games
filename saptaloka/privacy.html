Privacy Policy — Saptaloka

Last updated: 26 June 2026

Saptaloka is a single-player game that runs entirely on your device. It is built to respect your privacy by collecting nothing.

What we collect

Nothing. Saptaloka does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal or sensitive information. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising SDKs, and no tracking of any kind.

Data stored on your device

The game saves your progress (unlocked upgrades, best run, settings) in your browser's local storage on your own device. This never leaves your device and is not accessible to us. Clearing the app's data or browser storage erases it permanently.

Network

The game works fully offline once loaded and makes no network requests to send your data anywhere.

Children

Saptaloka collects no data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page.
